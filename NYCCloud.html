NYC on Cloud IX

A trip to New York City has got be high on anybody and everybody’s bucket list. No matter whether you’re there for 3 days of unsupervised debauchery, or you wake up everyday to its glory in your closet-sized apartment, your first instinct should be to keep your eyes street level. I mean, that’s where all the action happens, right? The cab that almost clips you. The bike messenger cursing out said cab. The dog poop on the sidewalk. The $1 pizza sign. Honestly, I could do this for 7 hours. I’ll save that for your visit. But with all that being said, the real magic actually happens above the rooftops. Where 100 year old buildings and cathedrals mingle with some of the most amazing modern architecture in the world, to a place where tenants can find respite from their daily street walking. From sun down to sun up this place provided the perfect petri dish for inspiration. The experience is never promised and sometimes a pricey one. Below are images from my time on Cloud IX.
