Look Hear Collective Gallery

Dupont Underground Washington DC 9/27/19

Summer 2019 I brought together 4 African American photographers from the D.C. area to create Look Hear Collective, a group whose work highlights the connection between the cultures of visual arts and music. On September 27th 2019 at Dupont Underground we had a gallery event that extended the conversation with presentations of our works, a speaking panel with our event partner The Washington Informer The Bridge, performing artist, vendors, and a live mural painting session. Myself, Abdullah Konte, Ja'Mon Jackson, and Jordan Henson will continue to tell the visual stories of our experiences with the community and culture. STAY TUNED! #LookHearCollective
